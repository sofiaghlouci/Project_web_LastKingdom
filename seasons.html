The Seasons

Season 1


Launched in 2015.

Number of episodes: 8.

On BBC, Netflix

At the end of the 9th century, what is called England today is separated into several kingdoms. The Anglo-Saxon lands were attacked and ruled by the Danes. Uhtred was the orphan son of a noble Saxon. Kidnapped by the Scandinavians and raised among them, he will have to constantly choose between the kingdom of his origins and the people who saw him grow up.

Season 2


Launched in 2017

Number of episodes: 8

On BBC, Netflix

Uhtred lost a lot in the battle led by King Alfred to save the kingdom of Wessex, and gradually begins to mourn. He then began a journey to the North in order to avenge Ragnar's death, and recover the land of his ancestors. As he arrived in the north of the country, plagued by chaos and rebellion, Alfred was convinced that England must be united. It was a dangerous time for the Saxons and the Danes but Alfred decided to fight for the new king, a Christian Dane, in order to unify the tribes. Uhtred questions his future and his loyalty, fighting for his country of origin, England, against the Vikings who raised him. Between love, rivalry, bloody battles and betrayals, Uhtred will be put to the test.

Season 3


Launched in 2018

Alfred's health declined, and with it his dream of a united England, Uhtred had to choose his side in confusion and face new invaders of Wessex.
