Uthred of Bebbanburgh

We can't talk about the Last Kingdom without talking about Uthred of Bebbanburgh. Played by Alexander Dreymon, Urthred is the heir of King of Bebbanburgh. But when he was little, his land was attacked by the Vikings more precisely by the clan of the famous warrior Ragnar. His dad died in battle and Uthred was taken as a slave by the Vikings because Ragnar didn't want to kill him. His venal uncle took the throne, and he chased Uthred to kill him and remain the only heir. He even asked Ragnar to pay him to take Uthred back but he refused and paid Uthred's uncle to keep Uthred. After that, Ragnar saw a light, bravery and boldness in Uthred so he adopted him and raised him as his son. So, Uthred grew up with vikings, he learned their habits, their religion, their langage and above all he learned how to fight like a Viking.
